*The Fundamental Law*

IR = Information Ratio(reflects performance, sharpe ratio of excess return) = mean(alpha) / std(alpha)
IC = Information Coefficient(reflects skills, correlation of forecasts to returns)
IR = IC * sqrt(breadth=numbers of trading opportunities per year)
